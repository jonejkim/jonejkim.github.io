Visualizes the idea of Discrete Fourier Transform from music waveform signals (in a friendly way..)


Music players with visualizations are fun to look at because it is aesthetically pleasing. Being an engineer with understanding of Fourier Tansforms and its relationship to audio signal processing, I was trying to find a visualization that catches both mathematical and aesthetic visualization of music, but was not able to find within my reach. So I created one myself for the sake of fun.
